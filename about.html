About

In May 2025, this website started as a passion project of mine when I got the idea of creating an all in one place for line dancing info relating to Ventura County and the hobby itself. As I started line dancing just over a year before, I had no idea about the different venues and what to learn. After searching around lots and trying line dancing more and more, I quickly enjoyed it and have met some great people doing so. This site includes what I've found in hopes that it helps others.

Goal

My goal of this website is to hopefully lower the learning curve of line dancing and share key info about it in one simple place. As I know it can be difficult to start without guidance, I hope this helps others get on the journey of line dancing. Hope to see you on the dance floor!
